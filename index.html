Choose Your Path

Two Tracks.
One Goal.

New this year: participants choose between two distinct competition tracks, each rewarding different strengths. Whether you're a natural experimenter or a born entrepreneur — there's a track made for you.
Research Track

The Deep Blue

Where rigorous inquiry meets real-world science.

  • A working model or research paper is required
  • Focus on scientific methodology and evidence
  • Demonstrate knowledge gained through experimentation
  • Present findings to expert science judges
Pitch Track

The White Cap

Where ideas become businesses worth funding.

  • Working model is optional
  • A compelling business pitch is the deliverable
  • Present to science and business judges
  • Creativity and market insight are rewarded

What You'll Present

The Display Board

Both tracks must have a professional display board that includes diagrams, illustrations, and clear explanations — simple enough for any visitor to understand. Font sizes must be large enough to read from a few feet away; smaller fonts are acceptable for captions and tables only.

Every board must include the following seven elements:

01

Project Name

02

Class(es)

03

Participant Names

04

Objectives

05

06

Results

07

Conclusions

White Cap Track Only

Your Pitch

Creativity is encouraged. Make your pitch as original and engaging as you wish — just make sure it covers all six required components below. The most memorable pitches tell a compelling story, not just a plan.
⚠️

The Problem

Clearly define the gap, pain point, or challenge your solution addresses.

💡

The Solution

What do you offer, and how does it directly solve the problem?

🏆

Unique Selling Proposition

Why yours? What makes your idea different from anything else out there?

📊

Market Size & Target Market

Who needs this, how many of them are there, and why now?

💰

Business Model

How does the venture make money? Subscriptions, sales, licensing?

📣

Marketing Strategy

How will you reach and grow your audience? Think channels, partnerships, campaigns.

How You'll Be Evaluated

Judging Criteria

A panel of science and business experts will visit your booth. Each group gets 5–7 minutes to present. Be ready for sharp questions — judges assess independently, and teachers cannot assist during judging. Top three places will be awarded across all projects and pitches.
Criterion Marks
Objectives5 marks
Creativity5 marks
Thoroughness5 marks
Knowledge Gained5 marks
Skill & Clarity of Presentation5 marks
Quality of Visual Presentation5 marks
Total30 marks

Non-Negotiable

Safety Protocols

🔬Standard laboratory procedures must be followed at all times throughout the project.
⚡Electrical apparatus must be built to standard electrical safety requirements.
🚫Hazardous substances, firearms, and weapons are strictly prohibited.
🔥No direct flames, explosives, highly flammable materials, or dangerous chemicals.
🌿If a living organism is used, its health and physical integrity must be fully maintained.
🔊Lights, sounds, and colours from exhibits must be reasonable and non-distracting.
